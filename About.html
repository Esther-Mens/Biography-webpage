The following list is a time line of Onassis Life

  • 1906 - born in Karatas, Smyrna, Ottoman Empire.
  • 1922 - He left Smyrna during the great fire of Smyrna in 1922.
  • 1923 - He went to Buenos Aires, Argentina and started working as a telephone operator.
  • 1929 - He recolated to New York and started his shipping businesses.
  • 1946 - Onassis married Athina Livanos,daughter of shippingg magnate Stavros G. Livanos and Arietta Zafrikakis, on 28 December 1946.The couple had become largely separated by mid-1950s.
  • 1950-1956 - Onassis had success whaling off the peruvian coast.
  • 1953 - In Monaco he started to purchase the shares of Monaco's SMB using the tax haven of Panama and finally took control of SMB
  • 1954 - Cancellation of the agreement between the Saudi Goverment and Aristotle Onassis to transport Saudi Oil on his tankers and "in case, to makethe agreement ineffective.
  • 1956 - Aristotle Onassis signed a contract granting him operational rights to the Greek air transport industry. Olympic Airways was founded.
  • 1957 - Onassis and Opera Prima Donna Maria Callas embarked on an affair despite the fact that they were both married.
  • 1966 - Rainer of Monaco approved a plan to create 600,00 new shares in SBM reducung Onassis's stake to under a third.
  • 1967 - Onassis court until he left Monaco
  • Onassis announced the launch of project Omega, a $400 million investment program that aimed to build considerable industrial infrastructure in Greece including an oil refinery and aluminium smelter.
  • Onassis ended his relationship with Callas to marry Jacquelline Kennedy,widow of u.s. President John F. Kennedy
  • 1975 - Onassis died at the age of 69 on 5 March 1957 at the American Hospital of Paris in Neuilly-sur-Seine, France, of respiratory failure, a complication of the myasthenia gravis from which he had been suffering during the last years of his life.
